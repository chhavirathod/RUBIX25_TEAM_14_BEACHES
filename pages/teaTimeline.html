Tea Kettle Timeline

3500 to 2000 BC
Bronze Kettles of Mesopotamia

People who lived in Mesopotamia used kettles made from bronze, which had decorated spouts.

1615 to 1868
Charles Stewart Smith's Kettle

Gift of Charles Stewart Smith, 1893

1891 to 1992
The Separate Chamber Kettle

The kettle took about 12 minutes to boil water because the elements were placed in separate chambers.

1956 to present
The Automatic Kettle

It was an eye-opener that led to the development of an automatic kettle that we know today.

1980 to present
Lidless Tea Kettle

It is one of the inventions that came without a lid and was filled through the spout.
