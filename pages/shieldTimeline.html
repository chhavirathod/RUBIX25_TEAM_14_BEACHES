Shield Timeline

5th to 7th Century
Round Shield

The most iconic shield shape of the Middle Ages, the round shield offered excellent all-around protection and was versatile in combat.

8th to 10th Century
Kite-Shaped Shield

Resembling a kite shape, this shield was popular among knights, providing ample protection and coverage.

11th to 12th Century
Heater Shield

Characterized by its flat-topped shape, knights and soldiers widely used the heater shield, offering good defense and maneuverability.

12th Century
Buckler Shield

Smaller in size, the buckler was a versatile shield that provided close-quarters defense and allowed for quick movements.

13th to 18th Century
Large Rectangular Shield

A large, rectangular shield primarily used by crossbowmen and archers, offering protection while reloading or taking cover.
